Our story

Bootlegger Coffee Company began in Cape Town in 2013 with a mission to serve exceptional coffee and wholesome food in a welcoming space. From one neighborhood café, the brand has grown into a nationwide favorite, recognized for its bold coffee, fresh meals, and signature black and green style. Each cup is brewed from carefully selected, sustainably sourced beans, roasted to bring out rich flavor. Beyond coffee, Bootlegger cafés have become community hubs—places to connect, work, and unwind. Driven by passion, people, and purpose, Bootlegger continues to deliver quality, authenticity, and memorable experiences across South Africa.

Mission & Values

We exist to craft memorable café experiences with quality coffee, thoughtful food, and service that makes people feel at home. Our values are: quality, community, and sustainability.

What we do

  • Small-batch roasting for freshness
  • Seasonal menu items made locally
  • Events, workshops and community collaborations
  • Serving all-day café favorites
  • Creating welcoming spaces that double as work, meet-up, and relaxation spots
  • Delivering consistency and quality across every café nationwide
  • Partnering with local suppliers to support South African businesses
  • Franchise opportunities that empower local entrepreneurs to grow with the brand
  • Eco-conscious practices, including recyclable packaging and sustainable coffee sourcing
  • Blending modern café culture with authentic neighborhood charm

From the founders Pieter Bloem and De Waal

Bootlegger was born out of a desire to create a neighbourhood café culture where quality coffee and good food are shared. We carefully roast beans and design spaces for connection.

Quality & Sourcing

Our beans are sourced from trusted growers and roasted to highlight origin characteristics. We work with suppliers who adhere to ethical practices and use packaging that reduces waste where possible.
